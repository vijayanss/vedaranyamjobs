Privacy Policy

App: Vedaranyam Jobs
Developer: Vijayan Subramaniyan
Last Updated: March 26, 2026

Welcome to Vedaranyam Jobs. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users in Vedaranyam and surrounding villages. This policy is provided by SSV Multi Services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identification Data: We use Google Firebase to collect your email address for account security.
  • Job Data: When you post a job, we collect the employer's name, work location, and description.
  • Contact Data: We collect phone numbers provided in job posts to allow job seekers to contact employers directly.

2. Public Disclosure

Important: Any phone number or name included in a job posting is made publicly visible to all app users. This is necessary to facilitate local hiring.

3. Data Deletion (Google Play Compliance)

We provide users with full control over their data:

  • Job Posts: You can delete any job post you created directly within the app.
  • Account Deletion: To permanently delete your account and all associated data, please email mailatvijayan@gmail.com. We will process your request within 48 hours.

4. Liability & Safety

Vedaranyam Jobs acts as an Intermediary under the Indian IT Rules. We do not verify the background of every job poster. Users are strongly advised never to pay money for a job or share sensitive documents like Aadhaar/Bank details via phone.
